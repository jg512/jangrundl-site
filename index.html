A Few Honest Words

I grew up in Rostock and mostly stayed. I'm doing a master's in computer science here, after finishing my bachelor's at the same university. What keeps me interested is the overlap between machine learning and software engineering, along with the harder question underneath both of them: what we should actually be building, and why.

My degree took longer than the standard plan. For a good part of it I was also working to help support my family, and I would rather say that plainly than tuck it away. It taught me how to do real work in the hours left over after a long day, and how to stay patient when things move slowly.

I enjoy teaching almost as much as building. Trying to explain something clearly is usually how I find out whether I really understand it. Most days I'm happiest with a concrete problem in front of me and a thick book waiting at home.
